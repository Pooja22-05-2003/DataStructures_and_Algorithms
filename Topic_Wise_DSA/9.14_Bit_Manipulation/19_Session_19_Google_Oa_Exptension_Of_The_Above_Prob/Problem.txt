You are given an array A[] of size N.
Find the sum of bitwise XOR of all the q qeuries , in which each query contain (L,R),find the sum of xor of all  possible triplets (A[i] xor A[j] xor A[k]) in the array.

constraints:
1<=N<=10^5
1<=Q<=10^5
1<=A[i]<=10^9
