Given two integers : 

Y and K ; find minimum possible such that Z ; such that 

Y | Z = K ; where “|” means BitWise OR 

If it is not possible then print -1. 

Example:- 

Y = 10
K = 15 

Output - Z = 5 
As (10 | 5) = 15
