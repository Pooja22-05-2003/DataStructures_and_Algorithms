Que. Find the minimum cost to do this  : In one operation ; you must select two 
adjacent numbers ; and merge them to 1 number ; the cost of doing so is the sum of
 both numbers ; do this till the array has only 1 number left. Find the min cost to do
  please. 

[5,5,8] = [10,8] = [18] = cost = 10 + 18 
[5,5,8] = [5,13] = [18] = cost = 13 + 18 

Answer is 10 + 18 as it's the way with min cost.
