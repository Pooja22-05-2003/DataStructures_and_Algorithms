Problem : Given two arrays A and B , both of size "N"; find the number of
pair of subsets {s1,s2} , such that max(s1)>=max(s2); where 
s1= Subset from Array A
s2= Subset from array B.


Constraint :
1<=N<=100000
1<=A[i],B[i]<=100000


As the answer is so large , so do modulo 1e9+7
