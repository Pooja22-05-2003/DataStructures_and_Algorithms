Given an array of size ‘N’; find the number of ways to partition it such that sum of each part is <=M ; 

1<=N<=100
1<=b[i]<=m;
1<=m<=10^10;

-> [1 2 3 4 4] 
M = 5 

Output :- 3
P1-> [1] [2] [3] [4] [4] 
P2-> [12] [3] [4] [4] 
P3-> [1] [23] [4] [4]
