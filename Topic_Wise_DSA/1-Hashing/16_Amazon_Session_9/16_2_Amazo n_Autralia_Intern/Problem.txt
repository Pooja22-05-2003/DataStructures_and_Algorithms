Given an array, count the number of pairs (nums[i],nums[j]) such that i<j and (nums[i]+nums[j])%x==0 
(means that sum of both the number must be divisble by 0)

example:
n=5
x=60
price= [31 , 25 , 85 , 29 , 35]


output :
3
pairs = (31,29) , (25,35) , (85,35)
