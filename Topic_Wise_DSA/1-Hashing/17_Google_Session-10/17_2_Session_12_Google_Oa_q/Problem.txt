You are given an array A of N integers. You have to find the number
of special Pairs in array A. A pair of two indexes i and j are called special
if the following 2 condition are  satisified.

1. i<j 
2. A[A[A[i]]]=A[A[A[j]]]

Find out the number of special pairs present in the array A.

Note - 1 based indexing is used.

constraints:

1<=T<=10
1<=N<=10^5
1<=Ai<=N
